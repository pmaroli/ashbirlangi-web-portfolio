3 Seasons Diorama

These are the same environment dioramas with different textures, I tried to create a different environment with each one. The floors were made in substance designer, the models were made in maya and zbrush and use displacement and normal maps. Renders were done in Arnold.

Programs Used: Maya, Substance Designer, Substance Painter, Photoshop, Zbrush, Arnold.
